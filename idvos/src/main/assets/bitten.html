Identity Trust Management AG herein referred to as identity.TM Standard Business Terms for the VIDEO IDENT process

1. GENERAL TERMS

  • These Standard Business Terms apply to the use of the identity.TM identity verification or driving license check service via the VIDEO IDENT procedure.
  • identity.TM provides its users an identity verification, driving license check or age check as part of its identification services pursuant to these Standard Business Terms.
  • These Standard Business Terms are exclusively applicable. Any contradictory or diverging standard business terms only become a component of the contract in the event their contents conform to these Standard Business Terms or they have been expressly acknowledged by identity.TM in writing. The foregoing applies likewise in the event identity.TM provides services without reservation with knowledge of contradictory or divergent terms of the users.

2. THE OBJECT OF THE IDENTIFICATION SERVICE COMPRISES

  • The establishment examination and verification of identity, possession of a driving license or the verification of the age of a user for the business partner on behalf of a business partner and/or user;
  • The transmission of data and information collected to the business partner; and
  • The storage of a user profile for purposes of a future age/identity verification or driving license check with the express consent of the user.

3. EXPIRY OF IDENTITY VERIFICATION

  • Verification and examination of a user's identity is performed upon request of an identity.TM business partner who either wishes to establish identity or possession of a valid driving license or is required to verify it based on applicable law (e.g. Anti-Money Laundering Act (GwG)) before a contract may be concluded with the business partner.
  • Verification of identity is part of an overall process based on a video conference between the user and an identity.TM employee. If needed, the video conference is recorded to preserve evidence.
  • In the course of the identity verification process, only data appropriate and necessary in order to verify identity, possession of a driving license or the age of a user is collected based on the relevant legal basis (e.g. Anti-Money Laundering Act (GwG), the Interstate Treaty on the Protection of Minors in the Media (JMStV) or the De-Mail Act). Data collected is then compared to the personal data related to the user provided by the business partner.
  • The type and scope of data collection and processing depends on the business partner's requirements within the context of applicable laws and regulations with respect to identity verification, driving license checks or age verification. Requested data generally includes, name, place of birth, date of birth, citizenship and a user's address. In the case of a driving license check, this also includes issue date, issuing authority, expiry date, driving license class, valid from, driving license class valid to, driving license class restrictions.
  • Depending on applicable laws and regulations, it may be necessary to examine the data referred to above on the basis of a valid, official identification document (e.g. personal identity card). In such cases, additional data included on the identification document may be requested. When performing verification, it may be necessary to photograph both sides of the identification document as well as the user by making a screen shot. This applies likewise in the case of a driving license check.
  • Upon agreement, the user will receive a centrally-generated transaction number (TAN) by email or text message during the video conference that may be used for one-time, unique assignment that must be entered immediately by the user during the video conference. Identity verification may only be concluded following successful entry of the TAN.
  • identity.TM will only confirm the user's identity to the business partner once the data required for purposes of identity verification, driving license check or age verification are complete and satisfy applicable legal requirements.
  • identity.TM notifies the business partner of the successful conclusion of user identification or of the driving license check and transmits all screen shots and other data provided by the user, and recordings, created as part of the identification process to the business partner upon request.
  • Use of the identification service requires prior consent of the user to the applicable data protection statement of identity.TM. The data protection statement expressly sets out the user's right to revoke consent at any time, whereby upon which use of the identification service is no longer possible for revocation.

The foregoing notwithstanding, identity.TM cannot assume any liability for the continuous availability of the identification service. In particular, the availability of the identification service may be impaired temporarily to the extent required in the interests of server security or integrity or to perform of technical repair or maintenance work.

5. REGISTRATION

  • Registration is made by completing an electronic form contained on the web site of the business partner, or on the identity.TM landing page, completely and accurately and then sending the form to identity.TM by clicking on the appropriate button. The user is then transferred to the identity.TM identification service web site. identity.TM is authorised to contact the user at a user-provided e-mail address in the event the subsequent identification process is interrupted or the user terminates a session prior to the start of the identification process and inform the user of additional steps needed for identification.
  • Successful registration requires the prior, express consent of the user to these Standard Business Terms and the separate identity.TM data protection statement.

6. CONSENT

  • By submitting the registration form, the user provides his/her binding consent to the provision of identification services and confirmation of his/her identity to the business partner as well as to processing, storing and transmitting his/her data on the basis of these Standard Business Terms and the separate identity.TM data protection statement.

  • In particular, the user undertakes to provide data relevant to identification truthfully and completely.
  • In order to provide the identification service, the user is required to have a device capable of Internet access including a web cam (video and audio). The user is responsible for ensuring that his/her device meets required performance specifications in order to complete the identification process.
  • The user undertakes not to disclose data it receives from identity.TM. The user must ensure that such data is only used by him/her and must secure such data against access by third parties, in particular minors.
  • identity.TM reserves the right to terminate the identification process at any time if the user violates his/her obligations as set out in these Standard Business Terms.

8. REMUNERATION
